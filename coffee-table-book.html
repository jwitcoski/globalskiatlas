How we’re making it

From data to print-ready layout

The book is produced from the same data that powers the Online Atlas. For each resort we export a map and stats, generate AI copy, then flow everything into Adobe InDesign or Scribus for design and print.

  • 1. Data — Ski-area datasets (resorts, lifts, pistes, terrain, elevation)
  • 2. Export — Print-ready map + “fun data” (stats, highlights)
  • 3. AI copy — Short paragraph per resort from the data
  • 4. Layout — Into InDesign or Scribus for final design
